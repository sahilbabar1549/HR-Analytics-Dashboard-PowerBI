## Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI project designed to provide actionable workforce insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. This dashboard helps HR professionals monitor employee demographics, promotions, retrenchment planning, job satisfaction, workforce distribution, and overall organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze workforce demographics and employee distribution.
- Identify employees eligible for promotion and retrenchment.
- Monitor employee satisfaction and engagement levels.
- Track service years and workforce tenure.
- Support data-driven HR decision-making.
